Privacy Policy

Introduction

The METU NCC Chemistry Student Club collects and processes personal data of members, potential members, event participants, and other individuals we interact with. We do this to provide the best service to our members and to achieve our club's goals. This privacy statement explains why and how we use your personal data.

Who are we?

We are the METU NCC Chemistry Student Club, a student organization at the Middle East Technical University Northern Cyprus Campus.

Which personal data do we process?

We process the following types of personal data: First and last name, student ID, email address, phone number, major, year of study, participation details (of events and/or meetings), and occasionally photographs.

Why do we collect personal data?

We collect personal data for the following purposes:

Membership management and services

We collect information to manage club memberships, communicate with members, and provide club services.

Event organization and participation

We use personal data to organize events, manage registrations, and communicate event details with participants.

Communications

We may send club newsletters, event invitations, and other relevant information. Members can opt out of these communications at any time.

Improving our services

We use data to improve our club activities and services based on member feedback and participation.

Your rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights regarding your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [club email]. Your rights include:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to object to processing of your personal data
  • The right to restrict processing of your personal data
  • The right to data portability

Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy or as required by applicable laws.

Website

Our website is hosted on [hosting service]. Please refer to their privacy policy for information on how they handle data collected through our website.

Data security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at [club email].

Social Media and Third-Party Platforms

Our club maintains presence on platforms such as LinkedIn and Facebook. Your interactions with these platforms are governed by their respective privacy policies. We may share club updates and event information on these platforms.

Photographs and Media

We may take photographs or videos at club events for promotional purposes. By participating in our events, you consent to be photographed and for these images to be used on our website and social media platforms. If you wish to have a photo removed, please contact us.

Cookies and Website Data

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this website may become inaccessible or not function properly.

GDPR Compliance

As our club operates within Cyprus, which is part of the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This means you have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, and data portability.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@metuncc.edu.tr.

Payment Information

For membership fees and event registrations, we collect payment information. This may include:

  • Payment amount
  • Payment date
  • Payment method

We do not store full credit card numbers or bank account details. All online payments are processed through secure, third-party payment processors. These processors have their own privacy policies and we recommend you review them.

We keep records of payments for accounting purposes and to manage memberships. This information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized club officers and faculty advisors who need this information to perform their duties.

If you have any questions or concerns about payment data, please contact us at contact@metuncc.edu.tr.
